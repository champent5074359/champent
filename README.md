# BusinessOS

## Project Overview

BusinessOS is a back-office platform for small businesses and SMEs. It supports multiple business types, including apparel stores, restaurants, and retail shops, while allowing each business to manage multiple branches from a single system.

## Vision

Make essential business operations simple, connected, and actionable: owners can manage products, inventory, sales, expenses, purchases, people, and business goals in one place.

## Features

- Multi-business and multi-branch workspace
- User and role management
- Product and category catalog
- Inventory tracking by branch
- Sales and sale-item recording
- Income and expense tracking
- Supplier and purchase management
- Business events and goals
- Operational dashboards and reporting (planned)

## Technology Stack

- **Frontend:** React + TypeScript
- **Backend platform:** Supabase
- **Database:** PostgreSQL (via Supabase)
- **Authentication:** Supabase Auth (planned; not implemented in Sprint 1)
